RECIPE OF THE WEEK:

The Birria Taco is juicy, cheesy and delicious - a true showstopper. It's rich and flavourful and will certainly cater to your tastebuds.



Ingredients:

  • Beef steak, cut into 15cm cubes
  • 2 medium tomatoes, cut in half
  • 1 medium onion, cut into 4 pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp sunflower oil

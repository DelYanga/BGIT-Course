HONEY GARLIC CHICKEN

Honey Garlic Chicken is full of tasty, sticky and garlic-y goodness! Wow your guests with this easy to prepare meal in less than 30 minutes


Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts, cut into tenders
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 40g of ginger, peeled and finely grated
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 6 tbsp soy sauce
  • 5 tbsp honey
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil of your choice
  • Cooked rice and veg to serve

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Tip the chicken into a bowl, sprinkle over the flour and some seasoning and toss until the chicken is evenly coated. Marinate for 2 to 24 hours

  2. Combine the ginger, garlic, soy, honey and half the lemon juice in a bowl. Heat the oil in a large frying pan or wok over a high heat and fry the chicken for 3-4 mins until lightly golden. Tip in the honey sauce and stir-fry for 10 mins, or until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has reduced enough to coat the back of a spoon. Taste for seasoning and squeeze over the remaining lemon juice, if needed, then serve with rice and steamed broccoli, if you like.
